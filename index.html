1. Set the board width and height, I do recommend playing on maps 1000x500 or higher.

2. Start drawing by clicking on the board or use one of the predefined shapes - Glider, R-pentomino, Diehard or Acorn.

3. When you are ready hit that PLAY button!

4. PAUSE when you want to add something in the middle of action or CLEAR if you want to start over.
